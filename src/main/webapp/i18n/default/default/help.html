Cats ‘N’ Dogs

Which of your furry best friends will bring you big wins? Cats ‘N’ Dogs gives you 6 opportunities to reveal symbols for each ticket. Find 3 identical symbols to win the associated prize. Find each instant win symbol to win additional prize instantly!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Cats ‘N’ Dogs is to find 3 identical symbols or instant win symbols.

Select each paw print to reveal a symbol. Or, press {REVEALALL}.

Find 3 identical symbols to win a prize. Each instant win symbol revealed, an instant win prize is awarded instantly. Reveal up to 6 instant win symbols in one game.

When all the paw prints have been revealed, play ends. A message appears indicating the game’s outcome.

{REVEALALL}

Press {REVEALALL} to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all items have been revealed.

Press STOP to continue the game in manual play mode.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
